Sponsors & partners

Meet the organisations behind BSides Kraków 2026

14 May 2026 · 8 min read · By the BSides Kraków Team

A community conference is a collective effort. The organisations below provide the venue, resources, expertise, platforms and reach that help BSides Kraków remain accessible to the security community.

The order follows the official sponsorship hierarchy published for the 2026 event. Within each level, organisations are presented in the same order as the event website.

Host & co-organizer

BSides Nerd Sponsor

AGH University of Krakow — Faculty of Computer Science

Our academic host and co-organizer provides the D-17 venue and connects the conference with Kraków’s research, student and technology communities.

Live conference hosts & organizers

BSides Nerd Sponsors

Antisyphon Training

A hands-on information security training provider known for practical instruction, community-focused learning and immediately useful defensive and offensive skills.

Black Hills Information Security

A cybersecurity services and education company combining penetration testing, adversary simulation, defensive expertise and extensive free community content.

Leading sponsors

Leading Sponsors

Nova8 Cybersecurity

A consultative cybersecurity company helping organisations build scalable security strategies across application and data protection, governance, compliance and business continuity.

Checkmarx

An enterprise application security platform helping development and security teams identify and manage software risk across code, dependencies, APIs and cloud-native delivery.

Contributing sponsor

Secure Habits

Secure Habits

A Netherlands-based security practice working where cybersecurity, artificial intelligence and international standards meet, with an emphasis on making secure behaviour part of everyday work.

Supporting sponsors

Supporting Sponsors

In Cloud We Trust (ICWT)

A Polish cybersecurity integrator selecting and implementing cloud-native protection across applications, networks, identities, endpoints and DevSecOps environments.

Unimus

A vendor-agnostic network configuration management system for automation, configuration backup, change management, auditing and compliance.

Notable sponsor

Blaze Information Security

Blaze Information Security

A boutique offensive security provider specialising in penetration testing, application and cloud security, network assessments and red-team engagements.

Supporter sponsors

Supporter Sponsors

Cycode

An application security posture management platform focused on software supply-chain visibility, developer workflows and the consolidation of application security risk.

DevSecCon Poland

The Polish chapter of the global DevSecCon community, bringing practitioners together around secure development and practical DevSecOps knowledge.

Community partners

Community Partners

InfoSecMap

A free, community-driven platform for discovering cybersecurity conferences, meetups, CTFs and community groups around the world.

Practical DevSecOps

A hands-on training and certification platform covering DevSecOps, application security, AI security, cloud-native security and software supply chains.

Collaborators

Collaborators

CyberSec Games

Creators of physical and facilitated games that make threat modelling, incident response, privacy and security teamwork engaging and memorable.

ZeroDay AGH

AGH University’s student cybersecurity club, connecting learners who want to deepen technical skills and grow within the security profession.

OWASP Poland

The Polish chapter of the OWASP Foundation, supporting open knowledge, projects and local collaboration around application security.

Women Go Tech

A community helping women start and advance technology careers through mentoring, learning, visibility and professional connections.

Sessionize

An event-management platform supporting calls for papers, speaker workflows, programme building and schedule publishing for community conferences.

Media partner

The CyberSecurity Review

The CyberSecurity Review

A print and digital publication connecting cybersecurity decision-makers with practical strategies, leadership perspectives and emerging solutions.

Thank you for supporting the community

Every contribution helps create the space in which speakers can share, villages can experiment and attendees can learn from one another. We encourage participants to meet the teams behind these organisations during the event.
