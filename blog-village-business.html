Business Village: turning security into decisions

29 May 2026 · 5 min read · By the BSides Kraków Team

Cybersecurity becomes meaningful when technical evidence leads to better priorities, stronger investment and more resilient organisations. The Business Village is where those conversations meet.

The theme: security as an organisational capability

The Business Village focuses on the space between a technical finding and a business decision. It invites participants to explore governance, risk, compliance, leadership, resilience and communication without losing sight of how systems actually work.

Good security strategy is not a collection of slogans. It connects threats and controls to real services, customers, obligations and operational constraints. This village creates room to compare how different organisations make that connection.

Core question: how can security teams translate evidence into decisions that improve resilience without creating unnecessary friction?

Conversations to bring into the village

  • How do you explain technical risk to leaders without oversimplifying it?
  • Which metrics reveal meaningful improvement instead of activity?
  • How should organisations prepare for NIS2, DORA, the EU AI Act and other evolving obligations?
  • Where should ownership sit when security spans product, platform, legal and operations?
  • How can smaller organisations make responsible choices with limited resources?

Who will find it useful?

The village is relevant to CISOs, security managers, founders, consultants, GRC specialists, security architects, product leaders and engineers who increasingly take part in risk decisions. It is equally useful for technical practitioners who want their findings to produce change rather than disappear into a backlog.

What to take away

Come with a real decision you are trying to improve: a difficult investment, a policy that is not working, a risk that is hard to communicate or a programme that needs clearer evidence. Listen for language and approaches you can take back to your team.

The exact village programme will continue to evolve. Check the official schedule before the event for the latest activities and timings.
