Women Go Tech Village: careers, community and visibility

8 July 2026 · 5 min read · By the BSides Kraków Team

Cybersecurity is stronger when more people can enter, contribute and lead. The Women Go Tech Village creates space for practical career conversations, meaningful connections and a more inclusive technology community.

The theme: making opportunity visible

Talent is everywhere, but access, confidence, networks and recognition are not distributed equally. This village focuses on the experiences of women building careers in technology and cybersecurity while welcoming mentors, allies, hiring leaders and community members who want to improve the field.

The goal is not a single definition of success. It is to make different routes into the profession visible and to share the knowledge that often remains informal: how to find a first opportunity, grow technical depth, ask for support, lead a team or return after a career break.

Core idea: representation matters, but durable change also requires access to knowledge, sponsorship, opportunity and professional networks.

Conversations to expect

  • Starting and changing careers in cybersecurity.
  • Mentoring, sponsorship and building a useful professional network.
  • Growing technical confidence without pretending to know everything.
  • Leadership, visibility and recognition for security work.
  • Creating teams in which different perspectives can influence decisions.
  • Practical allyship from colleagues, managers and organisations.

Who should take part?

Women at every career stage are central to the village, from students and first-time attendees to experienced practitioners and leaders. The space also welcomes mentors, recruiters, managers and allies ready to listen, share responsibly and help create concrete opportunities.

Come with one next step

Bring a question you have hesitated to ask, an experience that may help someone else or one introduction you can offer. A valuable village visit can be as simple as discovering a role you did not know existed or meeting someone willing to continue the conversation after the event.

Women Go Tech supports the conference as a collaborator. The detailed village programme will be published through the official schedule as it is finalised.
